Personal Experiences with Food Allergies

Emily

At a birthday party, Emily accidentally ate a slice of cake containing peanuts. Within minutes, her face swelled, and she struggled to breathe. Quick action from her friends saved her with an epinephrine shot.

Michael

During a family dinner, Michael's aunt unknowingly used peanut oil in a dish. His throat began to close, but his sister recognized the signs and rushed him to the ER, where he received treatment and got well successfully

Sophie

Sophie experienced her first allergic reaction at a restaurant when she was served a salad with hidden traces of shellfish. Her parents, who were with her, had to swiftly intervene with medication.

Alex

Alex's allergy to dairy became evident when he ate a dessert at a school event. He broke out in hives and experienced stomach cramps, prompting his teacher to call for medical assistance.

Jessica

Jessica's severe gluten allergy led to an accidental exposure at a friend's house. She ended up in the hospital, but the incident prompted her friend group to educate themselves about food allergies and take precautions.

Mugu

Mugu's Growing up with a dairy allergy meant I had to be extra cautious about what I ate. One time, at a friend's birthday party, I accidentally ingested a slice of pizza with cheese hidden under the toppings.
